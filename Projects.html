Fingerprinting Project as an Undergraduate Researcher at UCSD's Medical School

I dedicated the summer of 2022 to research at the Majithia Lab which specializes in the Endocrinology system. Continuous glucose monitoring provides real time measurements of glucose levels for diabeteic patients in order to determine preventative care before spikes. My project was centered around determining whether or not indivdual patients had glucose traces unique to them. Ideally, after determinging values for various features on the glucose traces per day I would have applied machine learning techniques, however I was limited by the dataset. While some individuals had trends in their days, others had a significant amount of variation. To reach a more reliable conclusion, I believe I would need access to a dataset where patients had at least a month's worth of trace.
